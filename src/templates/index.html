About Us

A Brief History of NSBP

A Cause to "Beelieve" In

We are the only non-profit that offers grants and funding for scientific research focused on bee conservation. NSBP is run by volunteers and 100% of donations go towards education, research, and community programs.

"After federal funding decreased at my lab, a colleague told me about the NSBP. I applied and was approved for a research grant within three months. If not for the funding from NSBP my work and my lab would have been shut down."

— Some Scientist, Bee Magazine

The NSBP got its start in 1969 when founders Tommy and Mark met and became friends at Woodstock Music Festival. They discovered a mutual passion for bees and beekeeping and decided to start a non-profit to help spread awareness and education in an effort to protect the craft. We just had an immediate connection over our love for bees! said Mark about their humble beeginnings. Since then, our efforts have grown from beekeeping to scientific research and preservation.
